# Vegetable Cheese Fritters

## Ingredients
- 200 g zucchini
- 100 g carrot
- 50 g onion
- 30 g rice flour (or any flour)
- 50 g light trappista cheese
- 2 eggs
- Salt and black pepper

## Instructions
1. Grate zucchini and squeeze out excess liquid.
2. Grate carrot and onion.
3. Mix vegetables with beaten eggs.
4. Add flour, grated cheese, salt, and pepper.
5. Weigh total batter and divide into 8 equal portions.
6. Flatten each portion in a pan.
7. Cook 3â€“4 minutes per side until golden.
8. Serve warm. One fritter equals one serving.

## Notes
One serving equals one fritter (1/8 of batch). Can also be prepared in a waffle maker. Suitable as a light snack or side dish.
