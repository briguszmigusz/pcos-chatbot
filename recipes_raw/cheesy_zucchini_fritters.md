# Cheesy Zucchini Fritters

## Ingredients
- 750 g zucchini (approx. 4 small)
- 2 eggs
- 50 g whole wheat flour
- 2â€“3 cloves garlic
- 125 g grated gouda cheese (optional but included in macros)
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Fresh parsley

## Instructions
1. Grate zucchini using large holes of a grater.
2. Salt lightly and let sit for 30 minutes.
3. Squeeze out excess liquid using a kitchen towel.
4. Mix in eggs, flour, minced garlic, cheese, and seasonings.
5. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat.
6. Spoon mixture into small portions and flatten.
7. Cook until golden on both sides.
8. Divide into approximately 16 equal fritters.

## Notes
One serving equals one fritter (1/16 of the batch). Can also be prepared in an oven or air fryer. Suitable as a side dish or light snack.
