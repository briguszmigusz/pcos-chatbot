# Double Chocolate Zucchini Muffins

## Ingredients
- 1 egg
- 100 g skyr yogurt
- 200 ml 1.5% milk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 200 g zucchini, grated and squeezed
- 60 g chocolate protein powder
- 130 g flour
- 20 g low-fat cocoa powder
- 75 g sweetener
- 1.5 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 50 g dark chocolate

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (428°F).
2. Grate the zucchini and squeeze out as much liquid as possible.
3. In a bowl, mix the liquid ingredients first.
4. Add the grated zucchini.
5. Stir in the dry ingredients one by one until smooth.
6. Chop the dark chocolate and mix in two-thirds of it.
7. Divide evenly into 12 muffin molds.
8. Sprinkle the remaining chocolate on top.
9. Bake at 220°C for 5 minutes.
10. Reduce heat to 180°C (356°F) and bake for an additional 12–15 minutes.
11. Check with a toothpick — if it comes out clean, they are ready.
12. Divide into 12 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one muffin. High-protein, fiber-enriched snack option. Stores in the refrigerator for 3–4 days and can be frozen. Suitable for portion-controlled inclusion in a 1,500 kcal daily plan.
