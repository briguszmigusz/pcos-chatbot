# Whole Wheat Bacon & Sour Cream Pizza

## Ingredients

### Dough
- 200 g whole wheat flour
- 100 g white flour
- 1.5 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp lactose-free sour cream
- 150 ml milk or plant-based milk
- 15 g fresh yeast

### Toppings
- 100 g bacon
- 100 g grated lactose-free cheese
- 150 g lactose-free sour cream (mixed with garlic and black pepper)
- 1 small red onion, sliced
- Garlic
- Black pepper

## Instructions
1. Activate the yeast in lukewarm milk.
2. In a large bowl, combine the flours, salt, oil, and sour cream.
3. Add the activated yeast and knead into a smooth, elastic dough.
4. Let the dough rise for 30–40 minutes.
5. Stretch the dough into an oiled baking pan using your hands.
6. Pre-bake in a preheated oven for a few minutes.
7. Spread the garlic and black pepper seasoned sour cream over the dough.
8. Add bacon and sliced red onion.
9. Sprinkle grated cheese on top.
10. Bake at 170°C (340°F) until golden and fully cooked.
11. Divide into 4 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one-quarter of the pizza. Higher fiber due to whole wheat flour. More calorie-dense option suitable for higher-energy days. Can be balanced with a light vegetable side to support blood sugar stability.
