# High-Protein Savory Cheese Crackers

## Ingredients
- 65 g flour
- 30 g unflavored protein powder
- 100 g skyr yogurt
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 20 g light cheese (for topping or mixed into dough)
- Optional: sesame seeds, caraway seeds
- Egg (for brushing, not included in macros)

## Instructions
1. Mix flour, protein powder, baking powder, and salt.
2. Add skyr and combine until dough forms.
3. Knead briefly until smooth.
4. Roll out thinly between two sheets of floured parchment paper.
5. Brush with egg and sprinkle cheese and optional seeds on top.
6. Score into small squares using a pizza cutter.
7. Bake at 200°C (392°F) for 10–15 minutes until golden.
8. Let cool, then separate into pieces.
9. Full sheet equals one serving.

## Notes
Full batch equals one serving. Savory, high-protein snack alternative to traditional crackers. Can be portioned into smaller servings if desired.
