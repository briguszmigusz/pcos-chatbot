# Baked Pancake Roll with Cottage Cheese Filling

## Ingredients

### Pancake Base
- 2 eggs
- 120 g flour
- 400 ml 1.5% milk
- 2 tbsp sweetener
- 1/3 tsp salt
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp baking powder

### Filling
- 250 g low-fat cottage cheese
- 100 g skyr yogurt
- 1 tbsp sweetener
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Instructions
1. Whisk eggs with sweetener, milk, and vanilla.
2. Sift in flour and baking powder and mix until smooth.
3. Line a baking tray with parchment paper and pour batter evenly.
4. Bake at 180°C (356°F) for 15–17 minutes.
5. Mash cottage cheese and mix with skyr, sweetener, and lemon juice.
6. Once pancake sheet cools, spread filling evenly.
7. Roll lengthwise into a log.
8. Slice into 8 equal pieces.

## Notes
One serving equals one slice (1/8 of the roll). High-protein breakfast or snack option that can be stored in the refrigerator for 3–4 days.
