# Mini Cinnamon Rolls (High-Protein Version)

## Ingredients
- 125 g flour
- 100 g skyr yogurt
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp sweetener
- 3 tbsp sweetener (for filling)
- 1 tbsp cinnamon

## Instructions
1. Mix flour, baking powder, skyr, and 1 tsp sweetener until a dough forms.
2. Knead by hand until smooth and workable.
3. In a small bowl, mix cinnamon and 3 tbsp sweetener with a small amount of warm water to create a thick paste.
4. Roll out the dough between two sheets of parchment paper into a rectangle.
5. Spread the cinnamon filling evenly.
6. Cut into 8 strips and roll each into a spiral.
7. Flatten slightly with your palm.
8. Spray lightly with oil spray.
9. Bake at 180°C (356°F) in an air fryer for 8 minutes, or at 200°C (392°F) in the oven for 10–12 minutes.
10. Turn halfway through baking.
11. Divide into 8 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one mini roll. Light, portion-controlled snack option suitable for sweet cravings within a structured 1,500 kcal meal plan.
