# Slow-Simmered Italian Bolognese

## Ingredients
- 500 g ground meat (7% fat)
- 1 onion
- 100 g carrot
- 75 g celery root
- 8–10 cloves garlic
- 500 g tomato passata
- 1–2 tbsp oil
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 100 ml milk or 15–20 ml cream
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Oregano
- Basil
- Paprika

## Instructions
1. Finely chop onion. Grate carrot and celery root.
2. Sauté vegetables in oil until softened.
3. Add ground meat, season with salt and pepper, and brown.
4. Pour in passata.
5. Add garlic, tomato paste, oregano, basil, paprika, salt, and pepper.
6. Add 1–2 dl water and simmer on low heat for 60–70 minutes.
7. Stir occasionally and add water if needed.
8. Finish by stirring in milk or diluted cream and simmer 10–15 more minutes.
9. Divide into 4 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one-quarter of the sauce. Pasta, rice, or cheese toppings are not included in the macros. High-protein base ideal for flexible meal pairing.
