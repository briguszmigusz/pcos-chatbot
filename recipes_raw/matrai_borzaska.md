# Mátra-Style Chicken (Borzaska) with Broccoli Basmati Rice

## Ingredients

### Chicken
- 300 g chicken breast
- 250 g potatoes
- 2.5 tbsp oat flour
- 1 tbsp sour cream
- 1 egg
- Salt
- Black pepper
- 2 tbsp garlic sour cream (for topping)
- Grated cheese (for topping)

### Serving
- Cooked basmati rice
- Steamed broccoli
- Fresh salad (optional)

## Instructions
1. Slice the chicken breast into smaller pieces and gently tenderize.
2. Season with salt and black pepper.
3. Grate the potatoes using the large holes of a grater. Lightly salt and let sit for a few minutes.
4. Squeeze out excess liquid from the potatoes.
5. Mix grated potatoes with sour cream, egg, and oat flour until well combined.
6. Coat each piece of chicken in the potato mixture.
7. Fry in hot oil until golden and cooked through.
8. Transfer to a baking tray.
9. Spread garlic sour cream on top and sprinkle with grated cheese.
10. Bake at 180°C (356°F) until the cheese melts.
11. Divide into 6 equal servings.

## Notes
Nutrition values are calculated per serving (1/6 of the chicken portion only, without rice and salad). High-protein main dish that can be paired with controlled portions of basmati rice and broccoli for a balanced meal supporting stable blood sugar.
