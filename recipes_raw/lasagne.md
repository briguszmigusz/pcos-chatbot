# Light Turkey Lasagne

## Ingredients

### Meat Sauce
- 500 g ground turkey thigh (or lean ground pork/beef alternative)
- 1 large tomato, diced
- 1 onion (100 g), chopped
- 1–2 cloves garlic
- 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
- 30 g tomato paste
- 80 g lasagne sheets (4 sheets)
- 20 g light cheese
- 1 tsp basil
- 1 tsp salt
- Black pepper to taste

### Bechamel Sauce
- 40 g light trappista cheese
- 125 g light cream cheese
- 75 g skyr yogurt
- 50 ml 1.5% milk
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 175°C (347°F).
2. In a pan, sauté onion, garlic, diced tomato, and ground meat until browned.
3. Season with salt, pepper, and basil.
4. Add canned tomatoes and tomato paste. Simmer for 5 minutes.
5. Prepare the white sauce by gently heating milk, cream cheese, skyr, and grated cheese until smooth (do not boil).
6. Layer half the meat sauce in a baking dish.
7. Add 2 lasagne sheets.
8. Spread half of the white sauce.
9. Repeat layers.
10. Top with 20 g grated cheese.
11. Bake for 20–25 minutes.
12. Divide into 4 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one-quarter of the full dish. High-protein dinner suitable for meal prep. Can be refrigerated for 3–4 days.
