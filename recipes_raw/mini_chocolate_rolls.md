# Mini Cocoa Rolls (High-Protein Version)

## Ingredients
- 125 g flour
- 100 g skyr yogurt
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp sweetener
- 15 g low-fat cocoa powder
- 30 g sweetener (for filling)

## Instructions
1. Mix flour, baking powder, skyr, and 1 tsp sweetener until dough forms.
2. Knead until smooth and workable.
3. In a small bowl, mix cocoa powder with sweetener and a small amount of warm water to create a thick paste.
4. Roll out the dough between two sheets of parchment paper into a rectangle.
5. Spread the cocoa filling evenly.
6. Cut into 8 strips and roll each into a spiral.
7. Flatten slightly with your palm.
8. Lightly spray with oil spray.
9. Bake at 180°C (356°F) in an air fryer for 8 minutes or at 200°C (392°F) in the oven for 10–12 minutes.
10. Turn halfway through baking.
11. Divide into 8 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one mini roll. Light, portion-controlled chocolate-flavored snack option suitable for sweet cravings within a structured 1,500 kcal meal plan.
