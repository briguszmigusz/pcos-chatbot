# High-Protein Bagels

## Ingredients
- 130 g flour
- 60 g unflavored protein powder
- 200 g skyr yogurt
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2–1 tsp salt
- Optional toppings: sesame seeds, cheese, poppy seeds (egg wash not included in macros)

## Instructions
1. Mix flour, protein powder, baking powder, and salt.
2. Add skyr and mix until combined.
3. Knead into a slightly sticky dough.
4. Divide into 4 equal portions.
5. Shape into balls and form a hole in the center.
6. Brush with egg and add desired toppings.
7. Bake at 200°C (392°F) for approximately 15 minutes until golden.
8. Divide into 4 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one bagel. High-protein alternative to traditional bagels. Can be divided into 5 smaller bagels for lower calorie portions. Suitable as a base for balanced breakfasts or sandwiches.
