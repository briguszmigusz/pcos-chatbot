# Spiced Chicken Thigh with Air-Fried Potatoes and Yogurt Salad

## Ingredients
- 150 g chicken thigh fillet
- 175 g potatoes
- 100 g mixed salad greens
- 30 g skyr yogurt
- 5 g mustard
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Paprika
- Ground cumin
- Onion powder
- Garlic powder

## Instructions
1. Season chicken thigh with paprika, cumin, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
2. Place in air fryer on one side.
3. Cut potatoes into wedges and place beside the chicken.
4. Air fry at 200°C (392°F) for 20 minutes, stirring potatoes at 10 and 15 minutes.
5. For oven method, bake at 220°C (428°F) for 25–30 minutes.
6. Mix skyr, mustard, lemon juice, salt, and pepper for dressing.
7. Toss salad greens with the dressing.
8. Serve chicken and potatoes with salad.
9. Full plate equals one serving.

## Notes
Balanced, high-protein dinner suitable for meal prep. Store salad separately if preparing ahead to maintain freshness.
