# Protein Pudding Pancakes

## Ingredients
- 200 g protein pudding
- 1 egg
- 50 g flour
- 1/2 tsp baking powder

## Instructions
1. Mix all ingredients until smooth.
2. Heat a pan over low heat.
3. Pour small portions of batter into the pan.
4. Cover with a lid and cook for a few minutes on each side.
5. Optionally add a small splash of water to the pan while cooking to create a softer, fluffier texture.
6. Serve warm.

## Notes
Full batch equals one serving. High-protein breakfast option that can be paired with berries or nut butter for additional nutrients.
