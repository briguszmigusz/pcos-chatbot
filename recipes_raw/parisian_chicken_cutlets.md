# Parisian-Style Fried Chicken Cutlets

## Ingredients
- 500 g chicken breast
- 2 eggs
- 3 tbsp oat flour (for batter)
- Additional oat flour for coating
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Garlic granules
- Small amount of plant-based milk
- Frying fat (not included precisely in macros)

## Instructions
1. Slice chicken breast into thin cutlets.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Mix eggs with oat flour, garlic granules, and a small amount of plant milk to form a thick batter.
4. Coat chicken slices in oat flour.
5. Dip into batter.
6. Fry in hot fat until golden and cooked through.
7. Divide into 4 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one-quarter of the recipe. Higher-fat fried option. Often served with rice and cucumber salad.
