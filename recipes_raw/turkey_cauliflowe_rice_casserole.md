# Turkey Cauliflower Rice Casserole

## Ingredients
- 1 onion
- 1–2 cloves garlic
- 500 g ground turkey thigh
- 500 g cauliflower
- 100 g rice
- 30 g tomato paste
- 500 g skyr yogurt
- 2 eggs (or 100 g 12% sour cream as alternative)
- 20 g light cheese
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 2 tsp paprika
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Instructions
1. Separate cauliflower into florets and boil in salted water for approximately 15 minutes until tender-crisp.
2. Cook rice: toast in a lightly oiled pan, add 200 ml water, simmer until absorbed.
3. Prepare meat sauce: sauté chopped onion and garlic, add ground turkey and brown.
4. Season with cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
5. Stir in tomato paste and 150 ml water, simmer for 10 minutes.
6. Mix cooked rice into the meat sauce.
7. Preheat oven to 180°C (356°F).
8. Mix skyr with eggs, salt, and pepper.
9. Layer: half meat mixture → half yogurt sauce → cauliflower → remaining meat → remaining yogurt sauce → sprinkle cheese.
10. Bake for 20 minutes.
11. Divide into 4 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one-quarter of the full casserole. High-protein, balanced dinner suitable for meal prep. Stores well in the refrigerator for 3–4 days.
