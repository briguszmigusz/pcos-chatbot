# Chocolate Walnut Oat Cookies

## Ingredients
- 1 banana
- 90 g rolled oats
- 60 g 100% peanut butter (or any 100% nut/seed butter)
- 30 g dark chocolate
- 30 g walnuts (or other nuts/seeds)

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 190°C (374°F).
2. Mash the banana thoroughly.
3. Mix in the peanut butter and all remaining ingredients.
4. Using an ice cream scoop, divide the mixture into 8 portions on a parchment-lined baking tray.
5. Flatten slightly and sprinkle with coarse salt if desired.
6. Bake for 10–12 minutes.
7. Allow to cool completely before serving.
8. Divide into 8 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one cookie portion (1/8 of the batch). Naturally sweetened with banana and suitable as a portion-controlled snack. Stores in the refrigerator for 3–4 days.
