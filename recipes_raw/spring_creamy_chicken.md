# Spring Creamy Chicken with Peas and Green Beans

## Ingredients
- 700 g chicken breast fillet
- 1 onion
- 2–3 cloves garlic
- 200 g frozen peas
- 200 g frozen green beans
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 180 ml 10% cream
- 20 g cornstarch
- 1 chicken stock cube
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Fresh parsley

## Instructions
1. Chop onion and cut chicken into bite-sized pieces.
2. Sauté onion in a lightly oiled pan.
3. Add chicken and garlic, season with salt and pepper, cook until white.
4. Add peas, green beans, 300 ml water, cream, and stock cube.
5. Bring to boil, then simmer covered for 10 minutes.
6. Stir in cornstarch and cook uncovered until thickened (approx. 5 minutes).
7. Finish with lemon juice and fresh parsley.
8. Divide into 4 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one-quarter of the recipe. High-protein, vegetable-rich dinner option. Can be paired with rice or potatoes if additional carbohydrates are desired.
