# Skyr Yogurt Bowl with Granola and Strawberries

## Ingredients
- 1 portion granola (approximately 83 kcal)
- 300 g skyr
- 150 g frozen strawberries
- Sweetener to taste

## Instructions
1. Add skyr to a bowl and mix in sweetener if desired.
2. Microwave the strawberries for 2â€“2.5 minutes.
3. Mash lightly with a spoon and pour over the yogurt (including juice).
4. Sprinkle granola on top.
5. Serve immediately.

## Notes
One serving equals one full bowl. High-protein, moderate-carbohydrate breakfast option suitable for stable blood sugar. Can be adjusted by scaling granola quantity.
