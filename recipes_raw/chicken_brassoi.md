# Chicken Brassói with Air-Fried Potatoes

## Ingredients
- 400 g potatoes
- 300 g chicken breast fillet
- 100 g frozen green peas
- 2–3 cloves garlic
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Onion powder
- Fresh parsley

## Instructions
1. Dice the potatoes into small cubes, spray lightly with oil, and air fry at maximum temperature (around 240°C / 464°F) for 15–20 minutes, stirring every 3–5 minutes.
2. Meanwhile, cut the chicken breast into similar-sized cubes. Season with salt, pepper, and crushed garlic.
3. In a lightly oiled pan over high heat, cook the chicken for a few minutes on each side until golden.
4. Reduce heat to medium, add green peas and approximately 50 ml water.
5. Cover and cook until the peas are tender.
6. Combine the cooked potatoes and chicken mixture in a large bowl.
7. Sprinkle with freshly chopped parsley before serving.
8. Divide into 2 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals half of the full recipe. High-protein, low-fat dinner option that stores in the refrigerator for 4–5 days and is freezer-friendly. Suitable for balanced blood sugar when portion-controlled.
