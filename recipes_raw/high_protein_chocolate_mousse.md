# High-Protein Chocolate Mousse

## Ingredients
- 12 g instant gelatin
- 30 g chocolate protein powder
- 250 g skyr yogurt
- 1 tsp sweetener (optional)
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
- 15 g dark chocolate

## Instructions
1. Add skyr, protein powder, gelatin, sweetener, and vanilla to a blender.
2. Blend for at least 2 minutes until smooth and airy.
3. Pour into a serving bowl.
4. Finely chop the dark chocolate and melt in the microwave in short intervals, stirring between each.
5. Spread melted chocolate evenly over the mousse.
6. Refrigerate for at least 2â€“3 hours until set.
7. Serve chilled.

## Notes
Full batch equals one serving. High-protein dessert option with a chocolate shell topping. Suitable as a filling snack within a structured 1,500 kcal meal plan.
