# Flour-Free Green Pea Stew

## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion
- 1–2 cloves garlic (optional)
- 500 g frozen green peas
- 200 ml water
- 500 ml 1.5% milk
- 12 g cornstarch
- 1/2 tsp salt
- Black pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley (optional)

## Instructions
1. Finely chop onion and sauté in olive oil with garlic.
2. Add 100 ml water, cook until evaporated. Repeat once more.
3. Add frozen peas, milk, salt, and pepper.
4. Simmer on medium heat for 10 minutes.
5. Blend one ladle of the stew and return to pot.
6. Dissolve cornstarch in cold water and stir into stew.
7. Cook 2–3 more minutes until thickened.
8. Add chopped parsley if desired.
9. Divide into 4 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one-quarter of the recipe. Can be served alone or paired with a protein source such as grilled chicken or boiled eggs.
