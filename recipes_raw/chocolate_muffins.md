# Extra Chocolate High-Protein Muffins

## Ingredients

### Muffin Base
- 55 g oat flour
- 40 g chocolate protein powder
- 120 g skyr yogurt
- 50 ml 1.5% milk
- 5 g low-fat cocoa powder
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- Pinch of salt

### Topping
- 100 g skyr yogurt
- 15 g chocolate protein powder
- 10 g low-fat cocoa powder
- Sweetener to taste

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C (356°F).
2. Mix oat flour, protein powder, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
3. Add skyr and milk and mix until smooth.
4. Divide evenly into 5 muffin molds.
5. Bake for 20 minutes and allow to cool completely.
6. Mix topping ingredients until thick and smooth.
7. Spread topping over cooled muffins.
8. Divide into 5 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one muffin. High-protein, low-fat dessert option. Suitable as a controlled snack within a 1,500 kcal meal plan.
