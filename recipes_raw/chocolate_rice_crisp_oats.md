# Chocolate Rice-Crisp Overnight Oats

## Ingredients
- 40 g oats
- 20 g chocolate protein powder
- 5 g low-fat cocoa powder
- 1 tsp chia seeds
- 75 ml 1.5% milk
- 100 g skyr yogurt
- 1 tsp sweetener
- Vanilla extract
- 10 g dark chocolate
- 1/2 rice cake (~20 kcal)

## Instructions
1. Mix oats, protein powder, cocoa powder, chia seeds, and milk.
2. In a separate bowl, mix skyr with sweetener and vanilla.
3. Layer the yogurt mixture on top of the oat mixture.
4. Melt dark chocolate in microwave with a light spray of oil.
5. Crush rice cake into melted chocolate and mix.
6. Spread chocolate mixture over the top layer.
7. Refrigerate overnight or at least a few hours before serving.
8. Full recipe equals one serving.

## Notes
High-protein, fiber-rich overnight breakfast option. Provides slow-digesting carbohydrates and sustained energy.
