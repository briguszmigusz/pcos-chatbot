# Creamy Broccoli Cheddar Soup

## Ingredients
- 1 onion
- 500 g broccoli
- 2 medium carrots
- 200 ml cream
- 700 ml vegetable stock
- 1 tbsp oat flour
- 50 g butter
- 100 g cheddar cheese
- Black pepper
- Salt (if needed)

## Instructions
1. Melt butter and saut√© diced onion until translucent.
2. Add oat flour and cook briefly while stirring.
3. Pour in vegetable stock.
4. Add grated carrots and broccoli florets.
5. Simmer until vegetables are fully tender.
6. Stir in grated cheddar cheese and allow it to melt.
7. Add cream and gently heat through.
8. Season with salt and pepper.
9. Divide into 4 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one-quarter of the soup. Rich, higher-fat soup option. Best paired with a light protein source or salad for balance.
