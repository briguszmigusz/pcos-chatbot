# High-Protein Baked Tortilla Egg Wrap

## Ingredients
- 1 large tortilla wrap (~190 kcal)
- 1 egg
- 120 g egg whites
- 25 g bell pepper
- 50 g tomato
- 50 g chicken ham
- 15 g light trappista cheese
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Fresh parsley

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C (356°F).
2. Chop ham and vegetables into small pieces.
3. Place tortilla into a round baking dish slightly smaller than the tortilla.
4. Whisk egg and egg whites with salt and pepper.
5. Pour egg mixture over tortilla.
6. Add chopped vegetables and ham evenly.
7. Sprinkle with grated cheese and parsley.
8. Bake for 15–20 minutes until center is fully set.
9. Serve warm. Full dish equals one serving.

## Notes
Single high-protein serving suitable for breakfast or light lunch. Can be served with pizza sauce or ketchup if desired.
