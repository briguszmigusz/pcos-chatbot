# High-Protein Pizza Rolls

## Ingredients
- 200 g flour
- 210 g skyr yogurt
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp oregano
- 1/4 tsp onion powder
- 1/5 tsp garlic powder
- 80 g tomato passata
- 40 g light grated cheese
- 30 g chicken ham
- Black pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 175°C (347°F).
2. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, oregano, onion powder, and garlic powder.
3. Add skyr and knead into a smooth dough.
4. Roll out into a rectangle between parchment paper.
5. Season passata with salt, pepper, and oregano and spread evenly.
6. Sprinkle chopped ham and grated cheese on top.
7. Cut into 8 strips and roll individually.
8. Place on a lined baking tray and flatten slightly.
9. Bake for 17–20 minutes (or 10–12 minutes in air fryer).
10. Divide into 8 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one roll. Savory, protein-enriched alternative to traditional pizza snacks. Suitable as a light meal or meal-prep snack.
