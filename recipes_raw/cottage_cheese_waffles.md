# Whole Wheat Cottage Cheese Waffles

## Ingredients
- 2 eggs (separated)
- 200 g whole wheat flour
- 60 g melted butter, margarine, or coconut oil
- 125 g cottage cheese
- 1 tbsp high-intensity sweetener
- 250 ml unsweetened almond milk
- 1 tsp baking powder
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions
1. Separate eggs and whip the egg whites into stiff peaks.
2. In another bowl, mix egg yolks with flour, melted fat, cottage cheese, sweetener, almond milk, baking powder, and salt.
3. Gently fold the whipped egg whites into the batter.
4. Lightly grease waffle maker.
5. Bake waffles until golden and crisp.
6. Divide into 8 waffles.

## Notes
One serving equals one waffle (1/8 of batch). Higher-fat breakfast option depending on fat source used. Can be served with yogurt, fruit, or nut butter.
