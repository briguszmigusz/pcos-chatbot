# Grated Apple Protein Pancakes

## Ingredients
- 30 g vanilla protein powder
- 50 g flour (white, whole wheat, oat, or gluten-free blend)
- 1 apple (approx. 130 g), grated
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- Pinch of salt
- Water (enough to form thick pancake batter)

## Instructions
1. Mix protein powder, flour, baking powder, and salt.
2. Stir in grated apple.
3. Add water gradually until thick pancake batter consistency forms.
4. Heat a pan sprayed lightly with oil.
5. Cook pancakes on low heat with lid on for a few minutes per side until set and golden.
6. Serve warm. Full batch equals one serving.

## Notes
Single high-protein breakfast portion. Naturally sweet from apple, with added fiber for improved satiety.
