# Flourless Sweet Potato Protein Brownie

## Ingredients
- 2 eggs
- 200 g cooked sweet potato (or pumpkin)
- 30 g chocolate protein powder
- 25 g low-fat cocoa powder
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tbsp sweetener (to taste)
- 25 g milk or dark chocolate (for topping)
- Pinch of salt
- Splash of vanilla extract

## Instructions
1. Pierce the sweet potato with a fork and bake at 175°C (347°F) for 20–25 minutes until soft. Let cool, remove skin, and measure 200 g flesh.
2. Add cooked sweet potato, eggs, protein powder, cocoa powder, baking powder, salt, sweetener, and vanilla to a blender.
3. Blend until smooth and pudding-like.
4. Pour into a small baking dish.
5. Sprinkle chopped chocolate on top.
6. Bake at 175°C for 20–25 minutes.
7. Let cool completely before slicing.
8. Divide into 8 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one slice (1/8 of the pan). Flourless, high-fiber option naturally sweetened with sweet potato. Suitable as a controlled snack or dessert within a 1,500 kcal meal plan.
