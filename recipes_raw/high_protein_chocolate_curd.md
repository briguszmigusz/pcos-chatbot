# High-Protein Chocolate Curd Cake (Bocisüti)

## Ingredients
- 240 g skyr yogurt
- 100 ml 1.5% milk
- 110 g flour
- 80 g chocolate protein powder
- 10 g low-fat cocoa powder
- 250 g low-fat curd cheese (túró)
- 1 egg
- 30 g sweetener
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp baking powder

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (356°F).
2. In a bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, protein powder, baking powder, and 20 g sweetener.
3. Add skyr and milk, mix until smooth. Adjust sweetness if needed.
4. In a separate bowl, combine curd cheese, egg, 10 g sweetener, and vanilla extract.
5. Pour the chocolate batter into a baking dish.
6. Spoon the curd mixture on top in patches.
7. Bake for 25–30 minutes, until the curd layer is fully set.
8. Let cool before slicing into 9 equal pieces.

## Notes
One serving equals one slice (1/9 of the cake). High-protein, low-fat option suitable as a controlled dessert or snack within a 1,500 kcal daily plan. Stores in the refrigerator for 3–4 days.
