# High-Protein Cottage Cheese Dumplings

## Ingredients
- 500 g semi-fat cottage cheese
- 3 eggs
- 25 g vanilla protein powder
- 140 g semolina (wheat or rice)
- Pinch of salt
- Sweetener to taste (20–50 g)

## Instructions
1. Mix cottage cheese with eggs.
2. Add protein powder, semolina, salt, and sweetener.
3. Refrigerate mixture for at least 1 hour.
4. With wet hands, form approximately 35 g balls (about 25 pieces).
5. Cook in boiling water until dumplings float to the surface.
6. Remove carefully and allow to cool slightly.
7. Serve plain or optionally coat with breadcrumbs, poppy seeds, walnuts, or yogurt.

## Notes
Nutritional values are calculated without coating.  
One serving equals one dumpling (1/25 of the batch).  
Can be stored in the refrigerator for 3–4 days.
