# Whole Wheat Poppy Seed Muffins

## Ingredients

### Dough
- 250 g whole wheat flour
- 45 g wheat gluten
- 2 tsp high-intensity sweetener
- Pinch of salt
- 180 ml warm water
- 1 egg
- 15 g fresh yeast

### Filling
- 150 g ground poppy seeds
- 35 g high-intensity sweetener
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 150 ml milk

## Instructions
1. Add dough ingredients to a mixer bowl.
2. Pour in warm water and knead until smooth.
3. Let rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
4. Mix poppy seeds, sweetener, lemon juice, zest, and hot milk into a paste.
5. Roll out dough.
6. Spread three-quarters of filling over surface.
7. Fold dough, add remaining filling, fold again.
8. Cut into 9 equal pieces.
9. Braid and shape into muffin forms.
10. Rest 20 minutes.
11. Brush with beaten egg.
12. Bake at 170°C (338°F) for 30–35 minutes.

## Notes
One serving equals one twist (1/9 of batch). Each piece contains approximately 22 g carbohydrates. Fiber-rich but higher-calorie bakery option.
