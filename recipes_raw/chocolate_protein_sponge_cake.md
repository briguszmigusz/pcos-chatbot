# Chocolate Protein Sponge Cake with Pudding Topping

## Ingredients
- 100 g flour
- 50 g chocolate whey protein powder
- 20 g low-fat cocoa powder
- 50 g sweetener
- 250 ml 1.5% milk
- 1/2 tsp rum or vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt
- 10 g baking powder
- 1 protein pudding (200 g)

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C (356°F).
2. Mix flour, protein powder, cocoa powder, sweetener, salt, and baking powder.
3. Add milk and flavoring, then mix until smooth.
4. Pour into a baking dish.
5. Bake for approximately 20 minutes. Check with a toothpick.
6. Allow to cool completely.
7. Spread protein pudding evenly over the top.
8. Slice into 8 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals one slice (1/8 of the cake). Moderate-protein, lower-fat dessert option suitable for portion-controlled inclusion in a structured meal plan.
