# High-Protein Cottage Cheese Crust Pizza

## Ingredients
- 250 g low-fat cottage cheese
- 2 eggs
- 35 g flour
- 30 g tomato paste
- 25 g light trappista cheese
- 75 g chicken or turkey ham
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Garlic powder
- Italian seasoning or oregano

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 190°C (374°F).
2. Mix cottage cheese, eggs, flour, and seasonings.
3. Spread thinly on a parchment-lined baking tray.
4. Bake for 20–25 minutes until set.
5. Mix tomato paste with salt, pepper, and herbs.
6. Spread sauce over baked base.
7. Add ham and grated cheese.
8. Bake another 10–15 minutes until cheese melts.
9. Slice into 2 equal servings.

## Notes
One serving equals half of the pizza. High-protein, lower-carb alternative to traditional pizza crust. Suitable for lunch or dinner.
