# Apple Cinnamon Granola

## Ingredients
- 100 g rolled oats
- 1 apple (approximately 175 g), finely grated
- 30 g walnuts, chopped
- 1/3 tsp cinnamon
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions
1. Finely grate the apple using the small holes of a grater (do not squeeze out the juice).
2. Add rolled oats, chopped walnuts, cinnamon, and salt.
3. Mix thoroughly until a sticky mixture forms.
4. Air fry at 170째C (340째F) for 15 minutes, stirring at the 5 and 10 minute marks.
5. Increase temperature to 200째C (392째F) for a few additional minutes until lightly crisp.
6. Spread onto a tray and allow to cool completely.
7. Store in an airtight container for up to one week.
